Assignment : Integrity and Anti-Corruption

Task

Video and poster

Theme

Corruption in sports

Reflection

  • Through this group assignment, I gained a deeper understanding of the importance of integrity, espacially in sports. By creating the video and poster about curruption in sports, our group explored how unethical practice such as match-fixing can damage the spirit of fair play and trust among athletes, coaches and fans. Such corruption can cause division in society, especially in Malaysia's multicultural community, because sports play a vital role in uniting people of different races in this country. Other than that, it can also negatively influence young atheletes who look up to their seniors as role models. In addition, this group assignment strengthened our teamwork and communication skills. Each group member took responsibility for different tasks, such as scriptwriting, acting, editing the video and designing the poster. I learned that cooperating with others is essential to finish this group assignment successfully. Overall, I hope the knowledge and values gained from this assignment will help me become someone with integrity and responsibility in the future.
